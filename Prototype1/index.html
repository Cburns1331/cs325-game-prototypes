Rock Golf

  • Originality: Combines rocks with golf! Ever want to be a golfer and geologist? This is for you!
  • Technical Merit: Your ball can disappear! Watch out!
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned how to change background, add music, add a sprite, scale a sprite, etc. Not having something like Eclipse for JS. Would make life much easier.
  • Prototype Assets: Music from: https://www.soundjay.com/free-music-1.html Golfball from: http://hddfhm.com/images/free-golf-ball-clipart-20.png Background from: https://opengameart.org/
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: I got my idea from the cards the professor chose, combined them for learning purposes. Didn't copy anything or anyone.
  • High Concept: Swing the ball around on your rocky surface while listening to some cool music.
  • Theme: Golf, Rocks/Geology.
  • Mandated Variety: The music has nothing to do with either, I was just feeling the vibe.
  • Prototype Goal: It works with the physics in arcade mode, I hope to learn more about other versions of Phaser physics.
  • Player Experience Goals: Chill out, listen to the music and move your mouse around.
  • Gameplay: Move your mouse around the screen or off the screen to your hearts content.
  • Strategies: More accurate the mouse movements, the better they will be.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: In a world where the terrain is all rocks and the only thing that isn't covered in rock dust is golf balls, swing 'em around and have fun!
  • Target Audience: Anyone who has a mouse.
  • Play Time: However long your attention span lasts.
